Payment processors

We currently support three payment methods: Stripe, Dwolla, and mailed checks. Any combination of these payment methods can be enabled for your events.

We've chosen our payment processors carefully to make running an event as seamless as possible. Sales from your event are transferred directly into your Stripe or Dwolla accounts, and our percentage comes directly to us! No hassle, no invoices.

Stripe

stripe.com

19 countries

2.9% + 30¢ on all transactions

Stripe allows users to pay with credit cards – like PayPal, and at the same fee.

Sensitive data is stored on Stripe's secure servers, and attendees don't have to leave our site or create any accounts!

Dwolla

dwolla.com

US-only

25¢ for transactions over $10

Attendees can pay with their Dwolla balance or directly from their bank account. On the plus side, the fees for Dwolla are dirt cheap – but they do require that attendees create a Dwolla account first.

Checks

No fee! Not even a Dancerfly fee!

Checks are mailed directly to you for processing.
You must create an account with Stripe or Dwolla if you choose to use them. Signup is easy, but it will take several days for your account to be verified so you can accept payments, so don't put it off!
