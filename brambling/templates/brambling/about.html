About Dancerfly

Dancerfly is a solid, easy-to-use event registration platform built by dancers, for dancers. Leave selling tickets to us; you go focus on making the rest of your event amazing!

What we offer includes:

  • Payment on our site with credit card, Dwolla, or check
  • Mobile-friendly design
  • Discount codes
  • Customizable attendee data downloads
  • Housing management
  • Multiple managers per event
  • Simple refunds
  • Optional account creation
  • Discoverability based on dance style
  • International support
  • A knowledgeable, professional, responsive support team

If you're interested in using Dancerfly for your event, or if you have questions, email us at hello@littleweaverweb.com.

Let us know if there are any specific features that you need. We may already support them – and if not, we’re always looking to improve the platform!

Thanks for checking us out. And however you handle registration – best of luck with your event!
