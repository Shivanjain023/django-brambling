Dancerfly is a solid, easy-to-use event registration platform built by dancers, for dancers. Leave selling tickets to us; go make the rest of your event amazing!

Easy checkout

Zero account creation, plus a human-friendly design and checkout process! Use credit cards, Dwolla, and/or checks, whatever is easiest for you and your attendees!

Easy events

From customizable data downloads for your attendees to financial reports and simple refunds, we make the event management process as simple as possible for you!

Easy teams

Have a team of people involved in your event? Great! Control their access to data and editing on a per-event basis, or give them access to all your events at once!
