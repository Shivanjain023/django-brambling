About Brambling

Brambling is an open source dance registration platform built by dancers, for dancers. We're still in the early stages of development, so we'd love your feedback! (There will be a feedback button in the lower right of your screen once you've logged in.)

Why open source? No single dance scene has enough resources (in terms of skilled volunteers) to truly focus on creating an application that meets all of their needs. As dancers, we've seen a variety of homegrown systems over the years, none of them quite polished enough for general use.

By providing a solid, easy-to-use alternative, we hope to save some time for local organizers and volunteers â€“ and if we're lucky, to get a hand in return to make this the most awesomest social dance event registration system ever!

Thanks for your help!
