International Support

Dancerfly can currently work for any country, with two caveats.

First, we do not currently offer the platform in languages other than English. We may someday expand into other languages; if you want to be kept informed, send us an email at hello@littleweaverweb.com. If you can't wait for our timeline, you can also contribute translations to our Github repository.

Second, our supported payment processors may not support your country. Stripe, our credit card processor, is currently available in 18 countries; Dwolla is only currently available in the United States. However, we do also support payment by check, which, while cumbersome, should cover most cases if needed.
