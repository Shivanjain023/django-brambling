Language

Dancerfly is only available in English right now. We might expand into other languages someday; if you want to be kept informed, or just want to let us know you'd totally use us if we supported your language, email us at hello@littleweaverweb.com.

Payment processors

Stripe, our credit card processor, is currently available in 19 countries; Dwolla is only currently available in the United States.

If neither of those options works for you, you can still accept payments by check. If that doesn't work for you either, and this is the only thing keeping you from using our platform, please email us at hello@littleweaverweb.com, and we'll see if anything can be done.
